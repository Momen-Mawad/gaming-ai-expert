**Kent**

==Biography==
Kent is a villager in Stardew Valley. He is a former soldier who returned to Stardew Valley after being away for many years. Kent lives with his wife, Jodi, and son, Sam.

==Trivia==
* Kent has PTSD from the time he served in the war.
* The chevrons on Kent's sleeve can be interpreted as a military rank insignia.
* In a dialogue that occurs on Fridays, Sam reveals that Kent used to be a garbage man when they lived in Zuzu City.
* In a dialogue that occurs on Mondays in Summer, Sam says that Kent took him on trips to the desert when he was younger.
* Despite liking eggs, Kent tells you during the Egg Festival that he isn't a big fan of eggs.

==Quests==
### A Soldier's Star
Kent will send you a letter on the 15th of Summer, year 2, requesting a Starfruit. The reward is {{Price|500}} and 1 [[Friendship]] heart.

### Help Wanted Quests
Kent may randomly request an item at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store. The reward is 3x the item's base value and 150 [[Friendship]] points.

### The Pirate's Wife
Kent is to be gifted the War Memento in The Pirate's Wife quest.
