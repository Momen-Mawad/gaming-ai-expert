# Green Bean
Seed: Bean Starter
Growth time: 10 days
Season: Spring
Experience points: 9 Farming XP
Sell price: 40
Edibility: 10
Color: Green

The Green Bean is a vegetable crop that grows from Bean Starter after 10 days and continues to produce every 3 days after maturity. You cannot walk through green beans at any stage of growth, but you can walk through dead Green Bean plants.

## Tailoring

Green Bean is used in the Sewing Machine with Cloth to create a dyeable Shirt159. It is a green dye when used in the Sewing Machine with a dyeable clothing item in the feed. It can be placed in the green dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house for use in dyeing.

## Quests

* The Green Bean may be randomly requested in Spring at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 120 and 150 Friendship points.
