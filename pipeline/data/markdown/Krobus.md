* **History**:
	1.0: Introduced.
	1.1: Now sells [[Return Scepter]] and [[Void Egg]]s and offers progress in the [[Quests#Dark Talisman|Dark Talisman]] quest.
	1.3.27: Added [[Sign Of The Vessel]] and [[Monster Fireplace]] to shop stock. Added "[[Quests#A Winter Mystery|A Winter Mystery]]" quest. Changed Tuesday dialogue.
	1.4: Added as a potential [[#Roommate|roommate]] if the player is [[Marriage|unmarried]]. Removed exploit allowing multiple purchases of [[fish]] and [[Cooking|cooked dishes]] by closing and re-opening the shop menu.
	1.4.4: Krobus no longer gets jealous.
	1.6: Added custom gift reactions. Krobus now loves [[Monster Musk]] and likes [[Strange Bun]]. [[Butterfly Powder]] and the [[Wizard Catalogue]] are added to fixed stock. The [[Dwarf]]'s [[Dwarf#50 Friendship Points|50 Friendship Points]] event (which involves Krobus) can no longer happen on Fridays.
	1.6.3: Added [[Krobus Portrait]].
* **Quests**:
	+ [[Quests#Dark Talisman|Dark Talisman]]: After the [[Wizard]] requests his [[Magic Ink]], the player must talk to Krobus, who will open the force field blocking the passage to the [[Mutant Bug Lair]], where the talisman can be found.
	+ [[Quests#A Winter Mystery|"A Winter Mystery"]]: Sent by mail the day after triggering a cutscene by entering the [[Bus Stop]] from [[the Farm]] during [[Winter]] between 6am and 4pm. To complete the quest, interact with a bush to the left of the [[Community Center]] in the north of [[Pelican Town]]. A cutscene ensues, during which a "Shadow Guy" (who may or may not be Krobus) gives the player a [[Magnifying Glass]]. This allows the player to collect [[Secret Notes]].
* **Bugs**:
	+ If multiple players open Krobus' shop in [[Multiplayer]] simultaneously, the limited quantity of items can be bypassed (multiplied by number of players). As an example, this allows two players on Friday to purchase two [[Iridium Sprinkler]]s instead of one, if neither player has already purchased the Sprinkler.

* **Information**:
	+ Krobus says that he normally does not sleep. However, he will fall asleep while watching any movie except [[Movie Theater#Movies|Mysterium]].
	+ While watching Mysterium, Krobus has negative dialogue about the depiction of shadow people within the movie. However, he still likes the movie overall and gains friendship from watching it.
	+ When drinking Void Mayonnaise in front of Krobus, he will respond by saying "Yummie!"
