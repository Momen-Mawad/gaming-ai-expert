**Fishing**

Fishing is one of the main activities in Stardew Valley. The player can fish in various locations around the valley, including rivers, lakes, and ocean.

### Treasure Chests

Treasure chests contain various items, including furniture, hats, and rare resources. The chance of finding a treasure chest depends on the distance from land and the luck modifier.

### Fish Frenzies

Fish frenzies occur when multiple fish are attracted to a specific location. These events can be triggered by using certain bait or tackle.

### Presents

Presents are gift boxes containing furniture or hats that can be obtained while fishing.

### Achievements

There are four achievements related to Fishing:

1. Fisherman: Catch 10 different types of fish.
2. Ol' Mariner: Catch 24 different types of fish.
3. Mother Catch: Catch 100 fish.
4. Master Angler: Catch every type of fish.

### Bugs

* On iOS and Android, there is a bug that affects the distance from land calculation.
* On Nintendo Switch and mobile platforms, casting the rod at exactly 2am can cause the game to fail to end the day.
