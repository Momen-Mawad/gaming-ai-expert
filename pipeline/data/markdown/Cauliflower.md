# Cauliflower
Cauliflower is a vegetable crop that grows from Cauliflower Seeds after 12 days. It's one of only five crops that can grow into a giant crop.

## Stages
Cauliflower can become a Giant Crop.

| Stage 1 | Stage 2 | Stage 3 | Stage 4 | Stage 5 | Harvest |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 Day | 2 Days | 4 Days | 4 Days | 1 Day | Total: 12 Days |
