# Artichoke
================

Artichoke is a vegetable crop that grows from **Artichoke Seeds** after 8 days. The seeds are available from Pierre's General Store starting in Year 2.

Artichoke can be acquired before Year 2 through the use of Mixed Seeds. Artichokes can then be placed in a Seed Maker to produce **Artichoke Seeds**.

## Tailoring
-------------

**Artichoke** is used to create a dyeable **Button Down Shirt** with **Cloth** in the Sewing Machine. It can be placed in the green dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house for use in dyeing.

## Quests
---------

* **Artichoke** may be randomly requested in Fall (year 2+) at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 480g and 150 Friendship points.
