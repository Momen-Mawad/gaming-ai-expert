# Pumpkin
================

A vegetable crop that grows from Pumpkin Seeds after 13 days. Pumpkin is one of only five crops that can grow into a giant crop.

## Stages

## Tailoring

Pumpkin is used in the Sewing Machine with Cloth to create a dyeable Button Down Shirt. It can be placed in the orange dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house for use in dyeing.

## Quests

* Caroline requests a pumpkin by mail on the 19th of Fall in the "Carving Pumpkins" Quest. The reward is 500 and 1 Friendship heart.
* Pumpkin may be randomly requested in Fall at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 960 and 150 Friendship points.
