**Evelyn**

Evelyn is a villager in Stardew Valley. She is a kind-hearted and friendly elder who lives with her husband, George, in their farmhouse on the outskirts of Pelican Town.

**Quests**

* **Granny's Gift**: Evelyn will send you a letter on the 15th of Spring, year 2, asking for a Leek. The reward is $500 and 1 Friendship heart.
* Evelyn may randomly request an item at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store. The reward is 3x the item's base value and 150 Friendship points.
* **Gifts for George**: On the Special Orders board in front of the Mayor's Manor during Spring, Evelyn may randomly request that the player collect and deliver 12 Leeks to the stove in her house. The reward is $2000 and a Coffee Maker.

**Gifts**

Evelyn likes the following gifts:

* **Cookie**: She loves cookies and will give you a special reaction when given one.
* **Leek**: She will ask for a Leek in her 1-heart event.
* **Zucchini**: She will like Zucchini and give you a positive reaction when given one.

**Special Interactions**

Evelyn has some special interactions with the player:

* When given a Cookie, she will give you a special reaction.
* When asked about George's health, she will tell you that he is doing well but has some creaky joints.
* When asked about her own health, she will tell you that she is getting on in years and has some aches and pains.
