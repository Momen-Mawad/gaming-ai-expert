**Mines**

The Mines are a location in Stardew Valley where you can dig for minerals and resources. The mines are 120 floors deep, with each floor having its own unique layout, enemies, and rewards.

**Difficulty Levels**

There are three difficulty levels in the Mines: 0, 1, and 2. Each level affects the stats of certain monsters, including their damage output and resistance to attacks.

* **Level 0**: The default difficulty level for the mines. Monsters have base stats.
* **Level 1**: Monsters have increased damage output and resistance to attacks.
* **Level 2**: Monsters have even higher damage output and resistance to attacks, making them more challenging to defeat.

**Dungeon Floors**

The Mines also contain dungeon floors, which are randomly generated and can be encountered at any depth. These floors often contain rare minerals or valuable resources.

**Mushroom Floors**

Some floors in the mines are mushroom floors, which are filled with purple mushrooms that can be harvested for gold and other rewards.

**Rare Drops**

In addition to the usual minerals and resources, certain monsters in the Mines have a chance to drop rare items, such as **Prismatic Shard** or **Diamond**.

**History**

The Mines have undergone several changes throughout the game's history. Here are some notable updates:

* **1.3.27**: Removed an exploit that allowed players to create a ladder to the Skull Cavern on floor 120.
* **1.4**: Added random dungeon floors and changed the way mushroom floors are spawned.
* **1.5**: Added the Shrine of Challenge to floor 120 after completing Qi quest.
* **1.6**: Raised the maximum Death Penalty from 5000 to 15000.
