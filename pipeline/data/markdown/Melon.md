# Melon
A fruit crop that grows from Melon Seeds after 12 days. It's one of only five crops that can grow into a giant crop.

## Stages
Melons can become a Giant Crop.
| Stage | Description |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | 1 day |
| 2 | 2 days |
| 3 | 3 days |
| 4 | 3 days |
| 5 | 3 days |
| Harvest | Total: 12 Days |

## Crop Growth Calendar
Not available.

## Gifting

Maru, Sebastian, Pierre, Caroline, Alex, George, Evelyn, Lewis, Clint, Emily, Sam, Marnie, Gus, Dwarf, Wizard, Willy, Krobus are neutral to Melon.

Penny loves Melon.

## Bundles
* Melon is used in the Summer Crops bundle.
* Five gold quality Melons are one of the options for the Quality Crops bundle.

## Recipes
Fruit Salad Recipe: 1 Melon, 1 Apple, 1 Orange
Pink Cake Recipe: 2 Melons, 2 Cloves

## Tailoring
Melon is used in the Sewing Machine with Cloth to create a dyeable Shorts. It's a pink dye when used with a dyeable clothing item.

## Quests
* Demetrius requests a melon by mail on the 20th of Summer for Crop Research Quest.
* Melon may be randomly requested at the Help Wanted board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 750 and 150 Friendship points.
* A fully-grown and unharvested Melon is required for Gourmand Frog's puzzle on Ginger Island.
