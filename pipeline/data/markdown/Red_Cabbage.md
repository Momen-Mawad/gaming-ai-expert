# Red Cabbage
================

Red Cabbage is a vegetable crop that grows from Red Cabbage Seeds after 9 days. The seeds are available at Pierre's General Store starting in Year 2, or can be found starting in Year 1 in the Skull Cavern or Traveling Cart.

## Tailoring
--------------

Red Cabbage can be used to create the dyeable Tank Top at the Sewing Machine. Male and female versions are available.

It can also be used for dyeing at Emily's and Haley's house, or 2 Willow Lane.

## Quests
----------

* Red Cabbage may be randomly requested in Summer (year 2+) at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of 780g and 150 friendship points.
