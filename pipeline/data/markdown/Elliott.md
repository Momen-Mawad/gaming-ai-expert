**Elliott**

==Summary==
Elliott is a 30-year-old man who lives in Pelican Town. He is one of the eligible bachelors and can be married.

==Dialogue==
###Before Marriage
{{quote|You've plucked Pelican Town's finest flower... Now, you must care for it with all your heart.}}

###Marriage
{{quote|Just look at our little family... We've come a long way, haven't we?}}

###Divorce
{{quote|Why do you torment me? Can't you see you've shattered all my hopes and dreams?}}

==Quests==
*Elliott may randomly request an item at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store. The reward is 3x the item's base value and 150 Friendship points.

==Trivia==
* In Secret Note #7, it is confirmed that he is one of three "older bachelors" in Pelican Town.
* Even though the letter for his 10-Heart event says to meet him "on a sunny day," it will still trigger if it is snowing.
* If Elliott is gifted Sea Cucumber or Super Cucumber, he will not accept the gift and will give it back to the player.
* On the day that the player marries Elliott, he may say, "You've plucked Pelican Town's finest flower... Now, you must care for it with all your heart." [[ConcernedApe]] revealed that this dialogue was intended to appear when the player marries Leah, but was mistakenly assigned to Elliott's own marriage event during data entry.[1]
