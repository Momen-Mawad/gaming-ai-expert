## Tailoring
Amaranth is used in the spool of the Sewing Machine with Cloth to create a dyeable Crop Top Shirt. It can be placed in the purple dye pot at Emily's and Haley's house for use in dyeing.

## Quests
Amaranth is requested by Marnie in a letter on the 3rd of Fall for the "Cow's Delight" Quest. The reward is 500g and 1 Friendship heart.
