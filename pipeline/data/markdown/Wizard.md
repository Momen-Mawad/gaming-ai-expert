**Wizard**

* **First Meeting**: Ah, yes. I have predicted your arrival a long time ago, young [Player]. However, your fate is ultimately in your own hands.
* **Regular**: Ah, yes. I have seen many things about your future, young [Player]. If I were to tell you, there could be grave consequences.

**Letters**

* The Wizard sends letters to the player with unique blue stationery.
* He may request that the player bring him a raw lizard egg sprinkled with blackened marrow at the Egg Festival.
* At the Flower Dance, he says "You shouldn't be up here."
* During the Luau, he comments on the merpeople's curiosity about human festivities and their upset when litter is thrown into the ocean.
* At the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies, he observes the Lunaloos' magical aura and mentions knowing Welwick from somewhere.

**Quests**

* **Meet the Wizard**: The morning after examining the golden scroll in the Crafts Room of the Community Center, the Wizard will send you a letter saying he has information for you. Visit the Wizard's Tower and he will give you a potion that allows you to read the language of the Junimos.
* **A Dark Reagent**: The Wizard requests that you bring him a Void Essence.
* **Staff of Power**: He asks for an Iridium Bar as a reward.
* **Dark Talisman**: After completing the Community Center Bundles or the Joja Community Development Form, trigger a cutscene at the Railroad, where the Wizard will request that you find a Dark Talisman. Obtain it from Krobus in The Sewers and place it on the artifact blocking the cave, which unlocks the Witch's Swamp.
* **Goblin Problem**: The Witch's Henchman blocks the way to the Witch's Hut. He only allows passage if given Void Mayonnaise. Complete this quest for access to the Dark Shrines inside the Witch's Hut.

**Trivia**

* The Wizard has unique dialogue for every festival, with some providing backstory on the events.
* His shirt can be tailored with 1 Cloth and 1 Void Essence, creating the Arcane Shirt.
