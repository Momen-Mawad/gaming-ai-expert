**Mining**

The **Mining** skill determines the efficiency of mining for resources. The higher the Mining level, the more resources can be obtained from each rock.

**Mining Skill**

* Level 1-10: +0% chance to obtain rare minerals
* Level 11-20: +5% chance to obtain rare minerals
* Level 21-30: +10% chance to obtain rare minerals
* Level 31-40: +15% chance to obtain rare minerals
* Level 41-50: +20% chance to obtain rare minerals
* Level 51 and above: +25% chance to obtain rare minerals

**Mining Buffs**

The following buffs can be obtained from food:

* **Mountain Chili**: +3 Mining, +1 Defense (7 minutes)
* **Crystal Cake**: +3 Mining, +1 Luck (7 minutes)
* **Cave Kebab**: +3 Mining, +1 Attack (7 minutes)
* **Hot Log**: +3 Mining, +1 Speed (7 minutes)
* **Earthy Mousse**: +3 Defense, +1 Mining (7 minutes)
* **Superfood Cake**: +3 Luck, +1 Mining (7 minutes)
* **Stuffed Mushrooms**: +3 Attack, +1 Mining (7 minutes)

**Food Recipes**

| Recipe | Description | Ingredients |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Banana Pudding | Temporarily increases Mining by 2. | Banana, Sugar, Egg |
| Eggplant Parmesan | Temporarily increases Defense by 1 and Mining by 1. | Eggplant, Tomato Sauce, Cheese |
| Cranberry Sauce | Temporarily increases Luck by 1 and Mining by 1. | Cranberries, Sugar, Water |
| Miner's Treat | Temporarily increases Attack by 2 and Mining by 1. | Peanut Butter, Banana, Honey |

**Gemstones**

* **Diamond**: +5% chance to obtain rare minerals
* **Emerald**: +10% chance to obtain rare minerals
* **Ruby**: +15% chance to obtain rare minerals

**Mining Nodes**

The following nodes can be found in the game:

| Node | Description | Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Diamond Node | Temporarily increases Mining by 2. | The Mines, floor 50 and above |
| Gem Node | Randomly drops a gemstone. | The Mines, floor 1-49 |
| Mystic Stone | Temporarily increases Mining by 5. | The Mines, floor 100 and above |
