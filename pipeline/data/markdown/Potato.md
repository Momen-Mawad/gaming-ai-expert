## Tailoring
A Potato can be used to create the dyeable Shirt And Belt with the Sewing Machine. It can also be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

## Quests
* Twelve Potatoes must be kegged for the "The Strong Stuff" special order.
* The Potato may be randomly requested in Spring at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store for a reward of $240 and 150 Friendship points.
