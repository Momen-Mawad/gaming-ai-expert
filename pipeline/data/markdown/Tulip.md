# Tulip
Tulips can be grown from tulip bulbs in 6 days. If grown within proper range of a Bee House, a tulip will cause Tulip Honey to be produced, increasing the honey base value.

You can purchase tulips from the Traveling Cart for $30.

## Recipes

## Tailoring

You can use tulips to create the dyeable Skirt in the Sewing Machine. You can also use it as a red dye in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.

## Quests

Tulip is not used in any quests.

## Notes

* Tulips have an equal chance of being one of five different colors.
* Due to its relatively small profit margin, it may be better to grow tulips for aesthetic purposes or use as gifts than for profit.
* Their cheap price, short growing period and reasonable energy restoration rate makes them surprisingly useful as early-game energy source.
