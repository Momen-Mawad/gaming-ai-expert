**Stardew Valley Wiki: Abigail**

**Introduction**
Abigail is a marriage candidate in Stardew Valley. She can be found at Pierre's General Store, where she works as a clerk.

**Trivia**

* Giving her something she likes will result in her implying that she is going to eat the gift, regardless of what it is.
* Abigail has a pet guinea pig named "David".
* Abigail has a poster of Crono hanging above her TV; Crono is a character from the Super Nintendo game "Chrono Trigger."
* Abigail has a bow in her hair on her sprite, but no bow is shown on her portraits. This bow can, however, be seen in early versions of her portraits.
* After the 10-heart event, during rainy days in floor 20 of the Mines, Abigail will say [player name] (85% chance), which is helpful when using the item ID glitch.

**History**

* Introduced in version 1.0.
* Added appearance in the Mines after viewing 10-heart event and added group 10-heart event in version 1.3.27.
* Added custom gift reactions, including liking Ancient Sword and Bone Flute, in version 1.5.
* Changed festival dialogue from "halloween" to "Spirit's Eve" in English in version 1.4.
