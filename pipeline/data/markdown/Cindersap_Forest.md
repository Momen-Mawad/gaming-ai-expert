**Cindersap Forest**

The Cindersap Forest is a vast wilderness area located to the north of Pelican Town. It is one of the largest and most diverse biomes in the game, featuring a variety of flora and fauna.

**Forage Items**

In the forest, you can find various forage items such as [[Spring Onion]]s, which grow only on tillable soil on the large southeastern island. Every night, any existing onions are removed, and new ones spawn.

The forest also contains a large variety of trees that can be chopped down to harvest wood and other items. As with all pre-existing trees outside the farm, they will automatically regrow as long as the stump is removed.

**Artifact Spots**

Cindersap Forest is home to many artifact spots, which can be dug up to reveal various artifacts. The forest is an excellent place to search for rare and valuable items.

Some of the artifacts that can be found in the forest include:

* [[Palm Fossil]]: 0.6% + 3-5%
* [[Prehistoric Scapula]]: 3-6%
* [[Ancient Doll]]: 2.4%
* [[Arrowhead]]: 1.9%

**Fishing**

Cindersap Forest is also home to several fishing spots, including the river and the pond.

The primary fishing location in the forest is the river flowing through it. It is an extension of the river found in Pelican Town, and therefore contains mostly the same fish. Two fish, [[Chub]] and [[Dorado]], are found in the forest river but not in town; the [[Smallmouth Bass]] is found in town but not in the forest river (it is, however, found in the forest pond).

The pond in the northwest corner of the forest contains a different selection of fish than the river. The pond fish include:

* [[Carp]]
* [[Catfish]]
* [[Midnight Carp]]
* [[Perch]]
* [[Pike]]
* [[Smallmouth Bass]]
* [[Walleye]]

**Waterfalls**

The waterfalls near the Abandoned House are also a great place to catch fish, including:

* [[Goby]]: available at any season and time
* [[Salmon]]: only available in fall

**Secrets**

By fishing from the southernmost point of Cindersap Forest near the entrance to the sewers towards the ocean, with a fishing level of at least 15 (only possible temporarily via buffs), an [[Iridium Krobus]] statue can be obtained.
