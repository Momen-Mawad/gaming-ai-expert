# Blueberry
Blueberry is a fruit crop that grows from Blueberry Seeds after 13 days and continues to produce every 4 days after maturity.

## Stages
Each harvest yields 3 Blueberries, with a 2% chance of more blueberries.
| Stage | Description | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1-5 |  | 1 day, 3 days, 3 days, 4 days, 2 days |
| Harvest |  | Total: 13 Days, Regrowth: 4 Days |

## Fertilizer
Basic and Quality fertilizers only affect the first berry from every harvest. Speed-Gro is an optimal fertilizer for this crop.

## Bundles
* Blueberry is required for the Summer Crops bundle.
* Five gold quality Blueberries may be an option for the Quality remixed bundle.
* Blueberry may be an option for the Dye remixed bundle.

## Recipes
Blueberry can be used in the following recipes:
* Blueberry Tart
* Fruit Salad

## Tailoring
Blueberry can be used to create dyeable Shorts at the Sewing Machine. It can also be used as a blue dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house.

## Quests
Blueberry is not used in any quests.
