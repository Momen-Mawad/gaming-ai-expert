= Demetrius =

==Summary==
Demetrius is a scientist and a villager in Stardew Valley. He can be found at his laboratory, where he is conducting research on various topics.

==Dialogue==
*When spoken to: "Don't mind me, [Player]. I'm just collecting some samples."
*At the [[Egg Festival]]: "Egg... what a strange thing to eat."
*At the [[Desert Festival]]: "Why do humans enjoy spicy food? It's counter-intuitive from a biological perspective. Unless there's something I'm missing..."
*During the day: "I'll probably get another tool set this year... *sigh*"
*When asked about his wife, Robin: "My wife is an excellent carpenter."
*When asked about his daughter, Maru: "My little girl is very clever. She's always tinkering with gadgets and machinery."

==Events==
*[[Quests#Crop Research|"Crop Research"]]: Demetrius will send you a letter on the 20th of [[Summer]] requesting that you bring him a [[Melon]]. The reward is {{Price|550}} and 1 [[Friendship]] heart.
*[[Quests#Aquatic Research|"Aquatic Research"]]: Demetrius will send you a letter on the 6th of [[Summer]] in year 2 requesting that you bring him a [[Pufferfish]]. The reward is {{Price|1000}} and 1 [[Friendship]] heart.
*Demetrius may request that you slay a certain number of [[Monsters]] or bring him a random item at the [[Quests#Help Wanted Quests|"Help Wanted" board]] outside [[Pierre's General Store]]. The reward for slaying monsters varies by the number/type of monster, but is always an amount of gold. The reward for an item delivery quest is 3x the item's base value and 150 [[Friendship]] points.
*[[Quests#Aquatic Overpopulation|"Aquatic Overpopulation"]]: On the Special Orders board in front of the [[Mayor's Manor]], Demetrius may randomly request that the player catch 10 season-specific fish. The reward is [[gold]] equivalent to selling the fish and the [[Farm Computer]] recipe.
*[[Quests#Biome Balance|"Biome Balance"]]: On the Special Orders board in front of the [[Mayor's Manor]], Demetrius may randomly request that the player catch 20 [[:Category:River fish|River]], [[:Category:Ocean fish|Ocean]], or [[:Category:Lake fish|Lake]] fish. The reward is {{Price|1500}} and the [[Farm Computer]] recipe.

==Trivia==
*If [[Robin]] is constructing something on your farm on the nights she and Demetrius go to [[The Stardrop Saloon|the saloon]], he'll go and dance by himself.
*Demetrius never mentions [[Sebastian]] in his dialogue outside of cutscenes.
*Despite Demetrius's six-heart event, [[Tomato]] is considered a vegetable crop by the game.
*If the player has successfully incubated a [[Dinosaur Egg]], speaking with Demetrius will result in him giving the player [[Triple Shot Espresso]].
