**Marnie**

= Marnie =

==Biography==
Marnie is a kind-hearted villager who lives in Stardew Valley. She is a breeder of various farm animals and takes great care to ensure their well-being.

==Personality==
Marnie is a friendly and caring individual who prioritizes the welfare of her animals. She can be quite chatty and loves to talk about her farm and animals. Despite being single, she has a soft spot for romance and often expresses her desire to find love.

==Quests==
* Cow's Delight: On the 3rd of Fall, Marnie will send you a letter requesting a single bunch of Amaranth. The reward is 500 and 1 Friendship heart.
* Help Wanted quests: Marnie may randomly request an item at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store. The reward is 3x the item's base value and 150 Friendship points.

==Trivia==
* When choosing the "Supplies Shop", there is a 0.01% chance for the text under Marnie's portrait to read "*sigh*... When the door opened I thought it might be Lewis."
* Marnie will still tell the player that Shane has lived with her for the past few months, even if they are married to Shane or have lived in the Valley for years.
