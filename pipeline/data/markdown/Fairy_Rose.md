# Fairy Rose
## Characteristics

| Property | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Seed | Fairy Seeds |
| Growth Time | 12 days |
| Season | Fall |
| XP | 29 Farming XP |
| Sell Price | $290 |
| Edibility | 18 |

The Fairy Rose is a flower that can be grown from Fairy Seeds in 12 days.

If grown within proper range of a Bee House (5 squares in any cardinal direction), a Fairy Rose will cause Fairy Rose Honey to be produced, increasing the honey base value from $100 to $680. This is the most valuable type of honey, compensating for the poor profit margins on the flower itself.

Fairy Roses can be purchased from the Traveling Cart for $290.

## Bundles

Fairy Rose is one of the options for the Garden Bundle.

## Tailoring

Fairy Rose can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the dyeable Long Dress. The fairy rose can also be used as a red dye pot color for dyeing, located in 2 Willow Lane, Emily and Haley's house.

## Quests

Fairy Rose is not used in any quests.

## Notes

* Fairy Roses have an equal chance of being one of six different colors. Fairy Roses of different colors will occupy different slots in inventory or chests. Six colors × four Crop Quality levels = 24 possible spaces used.
* Despite not having a true red variation, Fairy Roses are used as a red dye in the dye pots.
* Having at least one mature Fairy Roses on the farm by the end of a day increases the chance for the Crop Fairy event by 70% (from 1% to 1.7%).
