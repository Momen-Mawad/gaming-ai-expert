**Quests**

* **Robin's Lost Axe**: On the 11th of Spring, Robin will send a letter explaining that she lost her favorite axe, and offers a reward of 250 to anyone who finds and returns it to her.
* **Robin's Request**: On the 21st of Winter, Robin will send a letter requesting that you bring her 10 pieces of Hardwood. The reward is 500 and 1 Friendship heart.
* **Help Wanted Quests**: Robin may randomly request an item at the "Help Wanted" board outside Pierre's General Store. The reward is 3x the item's base value and 150 Friendship points.
* **Robin's Project**: On the Special Orders board in front of the Mayor's Manor, Robin may randomly request that the player collect 80 Hardwood. The reward is 2000, 250 Friendship points, and the Deluxe Red Double Bed for sale at the Carpenter's Shop.
* **Robin's Resource Rush**: On the Special Orders board in front of the Mayor's Manor, Robin may randomly request that the player collect 1000 Wood or 1000 Stone. The reward is 2500 and the Stone Chest recipe.
