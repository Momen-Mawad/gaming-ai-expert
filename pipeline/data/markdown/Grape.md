# Grape
## Overview
A fruit that is both a forageable item and a standard crop.

### Foraging
In **Summer**, Grapes are a forageable fruit found in the wild throughout Stardew Valley or can be grown from **Summer Seeds**. Specifically, forageable Grapes can be found at:
	* The **Backwoods** (62% of all forageable items)
	* The **Mountain** (62%)
	* The **Bus Stop** (27%)
	* The **Railroad** (27%)
	* Or, the **Forest Farm Map** (25%)

It can also randomly be found in **Garbage Cans** during Summer.

### Farming
In **Fall**, Grapes are a standard crop that grows from a **Grape Starter** after 10 days and continues to produce every 3 days after maturity. Grown Grapes receive the +10% Tiller profession bonus when sold (as do all crops). However, foraged Grapes in summer do not get the Tiller bonus.

### Stages
You cannot walk through grape plants grown from a Grape Starter at any stage of growth. You can walk through dead Grape plants.

| Stage 1 | Stage 2 | Stage 3 | Stage 4 | Stage 5 | Harvest | After-Harvest |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 Day | 1 Day | 2 Days | 3 Days | 3 Days | Total: 10 Days | Regrowth: 3 Days |

### Gifting
Grapes can be given as a gift to:
	* Robin, Demetrius, Linus, Pam, Jodi, Kent, Leah, Shane, Elliott, Harvey, Sandy
* Neutral reactions from Maru, Sebastian, Pierre, Caroline, Alex, George, Evelyn, Lewis, Clint, Emily, Sam, Marnie, Gus, Dwarf, Wizard, Willy, Krobus, Leo

Grapes can't be given to:
	* Abigail, Haley, Jas
* Hate reaction from Penny
* Love reaction from Vincent

### Bundles
A Grape is used in the **Summer Foraging Bundle**.

### Tailoring
Grape can be used in the spool of the **Sewing Machine** to create the dyeable Shorts. It can also be used in dyeing, serving as a purple dye.

### Quests
* The Grape may be randomly requested in **Summer** at the "Help Wanted" board for a reward of 240 and 150 Friendship points.

### History
* In 1.4, Grapes can now be used in Tailoring.
* Fixed how Tiller bonus applies when foraged and non-foraged fruit are combined in one stack.
* In 1.5, Iridium quality is now possible from grown grapes using Deluxe Fertilizer.
* In 1.6, It is now possible to process grapes into Raisins in a Dehydrator.
