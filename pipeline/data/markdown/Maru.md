= Maru =

==Biography==
Maru is a villager in Stardew Valley. She is a kind and caring person who works as a nurse at the local clinic.

==Personality==
Maru is a gentle soul with a strong sense of compassion. She is always willing to lend a helping hand, whether it's treating an injured animal or comforting a sick villager.

==Relationships==
Maru is a romance option for the player character, and she can be married. She also has a close relationship with her brother, who runs the local clinic where she works.

==Dialogue==
Maru has several unique dialogue lines that reflect her personality and interests. Some of these lines include:

* "I'm feeling excited for a new year of farming!": This line is spoken when the player enters Maru's farmhouse on a spring day.
* "It's wonderful to see all the plants and animals come back to life during the spring.": This line is spoken when the player talks to Maru in her farmhouse on a spring day.
* "I like to catalogue the different insects I see in summer.": This line is spoken when the player talks to Maru outside on a summer day.
* "Fall always reminds me of going out woodcutting with my mom... It might be that smell of freshly cut wood and sap.": This line is spoken when the player talks to Maru outside on a fall day.
